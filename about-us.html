About Uniglobe Overseas

PNUETECH is a brand of Uniglobe Overseas. Uniglobe Overseas is a leading Manufacturer of highly specialized design of Faucets and other Brass Accessories. The company was founded in the year 2006 and is well respected and appreciated in Sanitary Industry for their innovative ideas. The company was established with limited number of employees and now the graph of growth scale is increasing gradually. The results are the combine efforts of Our management, Engineers and Workers of Our company.

Our Vision

Our vision is also likely very similar to meet the above criteria and change the present scenario of sanitation industry by giving innovative products which are and will be Eco-friendly with indigenous designed by our highly skilled and qualified staff.

Our Mission

Our mission is to achieve these goals and satisfy our customers with best outputs.

Striving For Excellence

Three words that change how we think, create and produce: Form Follows Function. It is a principle that equates to a revolution. No form and no function can be considered in isolation. Not ending the developmental process until there's nothing left to be added or removed. Until the product is more than the combination of form and function. With this aspiration in mind, we develop, design and produce faucets, showers and accessories. For bathrooms and kitchens. Pioneering innovations with luxury.

Our Aspirations

We want to delight our customers with innovative system solutions for bathrooms featuring professional design, top quality and great user benets. We want to be the most appealing and preferred supplier, and achieve an internationally signicant market position through excellence and dependability, particularly when it comes to customer service. We want to achieve protable growth and sustainable stability for the company by delivering gratifying customer experience.
